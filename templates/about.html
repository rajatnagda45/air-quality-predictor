About AirSense

AirSense is a small AI project by Rajat — built with Python, scikit-learn and Flask. It predicts an estimated AQI using pollutant inputs and gives category-based advice.

  • Model: RandomForest (tabular regression)
  • Visualization: Chart.js
  • Hosted: Render (optional)

Use it as a learning project — extend with live APIs (OpenAQ) or LSTM time-series for forecast.
